        *   Admins can upload a completed W-9 on behalf of an employee via the All Employees table.
        *   Admins can download a blank W-9 form from the Add/Edit employee modals and the All Employees section header.
        *   A W-9 Review section to view, approve, or reject employee-submitted W-9s, with the ability to provide a reason for rejection.
