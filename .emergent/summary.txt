**PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS (Consolidated):**
*   **Core Functionality:** A full-stack application with a Black Bold theme, passwordless authentication, and distinct portals for Admins and Employees.
*   **Landing Page & Forms:** A themed landing page and various forms (Job Applications, Consignment).
*   **Admin Dashboard:** A comprehensive dashboard for managing employees, payroll, mileage, and messages, including a full messaging system.
*   **W-9 Management:** A complete system for handling multiple W-9 forms per employee. Admins can Preview, Download, Approve, and Delete W-9s.

*   **Geolocation for Clock-in/out:** The clock-in/out function for employees must only work when they are within a 0.5-mile radius of the coordinates . Admins are exempt.
*   **Auto Clock-Out:** Employees are automatically clocked out if they leave the work radius, with their time adjusted to the last verified in-range location.
*   **Time Adjustments:** Admins can manually edit time entries, either by adjusting clock-in/out times or by directly overriding the total hours, with a private note required for overrides.
*   **Centralized Reporting:** A unified Reports section in the admin dashboard for generating, previewing, and downloading Payroll/Shift, Mileage, and W-9 reports, with filters for employees and various date ranges.
*   **Data Attribution:** All data entered by an admin (e.g., mileage trips) should be attributed to a generic Administrator entity, not the individual admin's name.
*   **UI/UX:**
    *   The admin dashboard header should display Administrator instead of the logged-in admin's name.
    *   Payment-related sections should use the term Payment Records instead of Payroll Check Records.
